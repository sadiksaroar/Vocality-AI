Privacy Policy for Vocality AI

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Vocality AI ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

  • Purpose: The app requires camera access to allow you to:
    • Take photos for AI-based image analysis
    • Capture images for voice-based interactions
  • Data Storage: Images are processed and may be temporarily stored for analysis purposes
  • Third-Party Sharing: Images may be sent to our AI processing servers for analysis

Permissions Used

  • CAMERA: To capture photos and images
  • INTERNET: To communicate with AI services
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To access images from your device gallery
  • BILLING: For in-app purchases (if applicable)

How We Use Your Information

  1. Image Analysis: Photos taken with the camera are analyzed by our AI system
  2. Service Improvement: We may use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our AI models
  3. User Support: To provide customer support and respond to your requests

Data Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
  • Images are transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

Data Retention

  • Images are processed for immediate analysis
  • We do not permanently store images unless explicitly required by the service
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
